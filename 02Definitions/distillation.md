Defined Term — Distillation

“Distillation is clarity without full disclosure.”

Distillation is the authored extraction of the foundations layer from the primary record:
enough precision to understand and apply privately—
without exposing the full depth, mechanics, or source-chain.

Determined by Origin. Released as authored work.
Not crowdsourced. Not collaborative. Not derivative.
